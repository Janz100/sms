Sherubtse College Hosts Research Workshop and Guest Lecture on AI, Financial Literacy, and Socio-Political Theories

On September 25th, student leaders met with National Council members to discuss education reforms, focusing on course updates, AI in the curriculum, and career guidance.The meeting highlighted collaboration between lawmakers and students to shape modern, relevant education policies.

On Blessed Rainy Day, September 23, 2024, Sherubtse College hosted His Eminence Dzongsar Jamyang Khyentse Rinpoche for an enlightening session on spirituality and mental well-being, where he addressed the concept of "emptiness" and cautioned against attachment, highlighting the impermanence of material life. His Eminence encouraged students and faculty to interpret Buddhist teachings in modern contexts and warned of the rigidity of tradition. Afterward, he conferred blessings at the college's football ground, uplifting the Kanglung community.

On September 18, 2024, Frances Young, CEO of White Byte, visited Sherubtse College to discuss cybersecurity awareness. She addressed the dangers of cyberbullying, sharing real cases of blackmail involving fake photos, and warned about generative AI's role in collecting personal data without consent. Her talk underscored the need for vigilance and proactive cybersecurity measures in the digital world.
