On September 18, 2024, Frances Young, CEO of White Byte, visited Sherubtse College to discuss cybersecurity awareness. She addressed the dangers of cyberbullying, sharing real cases of blackmail involving fake photos, and warned about generative AI's role in collecting personal data without consent. Her talk underscored the need for vigilance and proactive cybersecurity measures in the digital world.
