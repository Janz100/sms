About Sherubtse Media Society

Empowering Voices, Inspiring Change at Sherubtse

Background Information

Sherubtse College is a thriving institute of education that raises its students to be capable contributors to the development of the nation. The administration encourages students to engage in both academic and non-academic pursuits that garner attention from within and beyond the community, celebrating accomplishments and applauding student growth.

In 2014, three units combined to form the Sherubtse Media Society (SMS), the collegeâ€™s primary media outlet and a platform for budding media enthusiasts. SMS comprises three sub-units: The Tower (news writing), The Photography Unit (photography and videography), and The FM (radio broadcasting). Each unit has its coordinator, all reporting to a single overall coordinator who answers to the staff moderator and the Office of the Dean of Student Affairs.

Mission

To serve as the primary media body of Sherubtse, capturing all activities within Sherubtse in the form of pictures, words, and sound, for both the college community and the outside world.

Vision

To stand as a quality media body that reflects and presents the standards of Sherubtse College.

Objectives

  • To provide media coverage for college and student-led activities, including photos, videos, and news reports for social media and weekly news updates.
  • To give an inside view of the Sherubtse experience.
  • To offer first-hand training and experience in media-related skills such as photography, videography, graphic design, video editing, news writing, and radio broadcasting.
